- **Tier 1**: `/pet-memorial-stones/` (planned), `/pages/reviews/`, `/memorials/pet-tributes/`, key product pages
- **Tier 2**: dog/cat category hubs, key guides, pet loss gifts pillar (planned)

## Pages That Should Not Be Overlinked

- **Deep archives**:
  - `/pages/reviews/page/{n}/` (keep minimal)
  - Potential future `/pet-tributes/page/{n}/`
- **Utility pages**:
  - tribute submit page
  - builder pages (keep focused on conversion flow)

Guidance:
- Prefer **contextual links** over nav stuffing.
- Review cluster pages should be **search-entry support pages**, not global-nav primary destinations.
- Keep 1 strong CTA per page section; avoid stacking multiple product CTAs.
