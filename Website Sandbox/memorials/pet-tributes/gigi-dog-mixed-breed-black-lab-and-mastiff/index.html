In Loving Memory of

Gigi – Mixed breed, black lab and mastiff Dog Memorial Tribute

A loving tribute to Gigi, a cherished Mixed breed, black lab and mastiff remembered with love by Deborah.

2023 - 2026

Shared by Deborah, MI

She was 8 1/2 years old when I rescued her. She had been at the shelter for 8 months because nobody wanted a senior dog. I took her and gave her, her best life. She slept on my couch with a pillow. She collapsed one day and never moved again. She was 11. I gave her a spoiled life, homemade dog food and all. The neighbors kids and mailman gave her treats constantly
